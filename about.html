Simpli Piano

Learn to play the piano the simple way — one note at a time.

Simpli Piano is a friendly way to start playing the piano, even if you have never touched one before. It shows you which note to play, you play it, and it tells you right away whether you got it — so you learn as you go.

You can play on a real piano or keyboard and let the app listen through the microphone and grade your notes, or simply tap the keys on the screen. Each note you need is shown two ways at once: on the music staff and lit up on the on-screen piano, so you learn where every note lives.

Inside you'll find a step-by-step Course, a note-reading trainer, play-along songs with falling notes, a free play piano, and a place to type in your own songs.

It installs to your home screen and works offline — so you can practice anywhere, even on a plane. Your progress is saved on your device; there's no account and no sign-in.
