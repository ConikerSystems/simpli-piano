Simpli Piano

Learn to play the piano, one note at a time.

A simple, friendly way to start — even if you've never played.

What Simpli Piano Does

Simpli Piano shows you which note to play, you play it, and it tells you right away whether you got it — so you learn as you go. Every note appears two ways at once: on the music staff and lit up on the on-screen piano, so you learn where each note lives.

You can play on a real piano or keyboard and let the app listen through the microphone and grade your notes, or simply tap the keys on the screen.

What's Inside

🎓 Course
Step-by-step lessons that unlock as you go — from finding the keys to playing real songs.
📖 Read Notes
A note-reading trainer: see a note on the staff, play the matching key, build your speed.
🎵 Songs
Play-along songs with falling notes and color-coded feedback, in practice or in time.
🎹 Free Play
A full piano to explore and play around on, any time.
✏️ Add a Song
Type in your own songs and practice them like any other lesson.
⭐ Your progress
Stars and progress are saved right on your device — no account, no sign-in.

Who It's For

🎹

👨‍👩‍👧

Private & Offline

Simpli Piano installs to your home screen and works fully offline. Everything runs on your device — there's no account, no sign-in, and nothing is sent to a server. Your progress stays with you.
