Simpli Piano

Learn to play the piano, one note at a time.

A simple, friendly way to start — even if you've never played.

What Simpli Piano Does

Simpli Piano shows you which note to play, you play it, and it tells you right away whether you got it — so you learn as you go. Every note appears two ways at once: on the music staff and lit up on the on-screen piano, so you learn where each note lives.

You can play on a real piano and let the app listen through the microphone and grade your notes, or play right on your iPad by tapping the keys on the screen.

Private, Offline & Ad-Free

Once it's on your home screen, Simpli Piano works fully offline — you can play with no internet connection at all (perfect for a plane). Everything runs on your device: no account, no sign-in, and nothing is sent to a server.

And because it's privately developed by Coniker Systems™, there are no ads — ever.

Get Simpli Piano on Your iPad

It's a free web app — nothing to buy, no App Store needed. Here's how to add it to your iPad or iPhone so it opens like a normal app and works offline:

https://jconiker.github.io/simpli-piano/
  1. Open that link in Safari on your iPad or iPhone.
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow at the top).
  3. Choose “Add to Home Screen.”
  4. Tap Add — a Simpli Piano icon appears on your home screen.
  5. Open it from the home screen. It runs full-screen and works offline.

Want to send it to someone? Tap Share at the top of this page to text or email them the link and these instructions.

Getting New Features

New features are released regularly. To get the latest version, just close Simpli Piano and reopen it while connected to Wi-Fi — it updates itself automatically, and your progress is kept. You can check the version number at the bottom of any screen.

Have an idea or found a problem? Tap 💬 Feedback on the home screen to send it straight to us — your feedback shapes what gets built next.
